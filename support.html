Getting started

Plume does not include games or a retail BIOS. Add your own game files to Documents/Games/ through the Files app, AirDrop, or Share → Plume. Supported formats include GDI, CDI, CHD, CUE, BIN, ZIP, and ELF.

Retail BIOS is optional. Turn on Fast Boot in Settings, or add dc_boot.bin and dc_flash.bin to Documents/bios/ from hardware you own.

Requirements

Plume is iPhone only. Requires iOS 18 and iPhone 13 or newer.
