Purchasing

Plume is a one-time paid download on the App Store. There is no subscription and no account required to use the app.

Help → Tip Developer is an optional In-App Purchase. It does not unlock features. You already own the full app after the App Store purchase.

Plume does not sell games, BIOS files, or other copyrighted content. You provide your own files.

Getting started

Add your game files to Documents/Games/ through the Files app, AirDrop, or Share → Plume. Supported formats include GDI, CDI, CHD, CUE, BIN, ZIP, and ELF.

Retail BIOS is optional. Turn on Fast Boot in Settings, or add dc_boot.bin and dc_flash.bin to Documents/bios/ from hardware you own.

RetroAchievements

RetroAchievements sign-in is optional and free. Accounts are created on retroachievements.org, not in Plume. Plume does not charge for RetroAchievements access.

Requirements

Plume is iPhone only. Requires iOS 18 and iPhone 13 or newer.
