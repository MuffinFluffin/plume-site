Privacy Policy

Plume - Dreamcast Emulator stores your imported games, saves, settings, and logs on your device. Plume does not sell your data.

Data on your device

Game files, BIOS files, save states, screenshots, recordings, and app settings stay in the app's Documents folder unless you delete them or remove the app.

Diagnostics

The app may write session logs and crash diagnostics locally to help troubleshoot issues. These files remain on your device under Documents/logs/ unless you delete them.

Third-party services

RetroAchievements sign-in is optional. If you use it, account data is handled by RetroAchievements according to their own privacy policy.

Legal

Plume does not distribute ROMs, BIOS files, or other copyrighted content. You are responsible for content you import.
